## Workflow Approval Process

### Approval Stages
1. **Review** - Product owner reviews feedback items
2. **Prioritization** - Items categorized by urgency and impact
3. **Technical Assessment** - Development team evaluates feasibility
4. **Implementation Planning** - Timeline and resource allocation
5. **Execution** - Development work
6. **Testing & Validation** - QA verification
7. **Client Approval** - Final sign-off

### Approval Matrix
| Role | Responsibility | Approval Authority |
|------|----------------|-------------------|
| Product Owner | Requirements validation | Must approve all items |
| Technical Lead | Feasibility assessment | Technical approval |
| Project Manager | Timeline & resources | Implementation approval |
| QA Lead | Testing verification | Quality approval |
| Client Representative | Final acceptance | Final sign-off |
